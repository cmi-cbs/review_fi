# Bond Math Review

An interactive web app to help MBA students understand bond pricing fundamentals.

## Sections

1. **Why Bond Math?** - Real-world use cases for portfolio managers, treasurers, and traders
2. **Spot Rate vs. Yield** - The key conceptual distinction
3. **Computing Bond Prices** - From spot rates with semi-annual compounding
4. **Computing Yields** - YTM calculation from prices

## For CMI Students

This app accompanies Session 4 (Fixed Income - Bond Pricing & Interest Rates) of the Capital Markets & Investments course at Columbia Business School.
